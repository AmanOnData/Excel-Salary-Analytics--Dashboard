💼 Salary Analytics Dashboard

An interactive Salary Analytics Dashboard designed to analyze employee salary distribution, department-wise allocation, job role insights, gender comparison, and experience trends.

This dashboard helps HR teams and management make strategic decisions related to compensation planning and workforce distribution.

📊 Project Overview

The Salary Analytics Dashboard provides insights into:

💰 Total Salary Distribution

👥 Total Employees

🏢 Salary by Department

🌆 Salary by City

👩‍💼 Salary by Gender

📌 Employees by Department & Job Role

📈 Employees by Experience
